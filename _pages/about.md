## About

I am a Ph.D. student in the [School of Science and Engineering](https://sse.cuhk.edu.cn/), [the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen](https://www.cuhk.edu.cn/), advised by [Prof. Tinghuan Chen](https://mypage.cuhk.edu.cn/academics/chentinghuan/).

My research interests focus on the interdisciplinary area of **analog integrated circuit design automation** and **artificial intelligence**, including:
- Graph Neural Networks and Circuit Representation Learning
- Reinforcement Learning in Layout Design
- Large Language Models for Analog EDA
- Optimization Algorithms for Circuit Design Automation
